Environment Variables for Railway Dashboard:

ENVIRONMENT=production
PYTHONUNBUFFERED=1
PYTHONDONTWRITEBYTECODE=1
LOG_LEVEL=INFO
MAX_CONCURRENT_DOWNLOADS=3
ENABLE_CORS=true
DATABASE_PATH=/app/data/andalus_downloader.db
DOWNLOADS_PATH=/app/downloads

Instructions:
1. Go to railway.app
2. Open your andalus-downloader project
3. Click on "Variables" tab
4. Add each variable above (one per line)
5. Railway will automatically redeploy with new variables
